Latte

A single shot of espresso to three parts of steamed milk.

Espresso

A strong black coffee made by forcing steam through dark-roast aromatic coffee beans at high pressure in an espresso machine.

Cappuccino

A combination of equal parts espresso, steamed milk and milk froth. This luxurious drink, if made properly, can double as a dessert with its complex flavors and richness.

Americano

A single shot of espresso added to a cup of hot water.

White Tea

White teas are picked when young tea buds are tightly enclosed in new leaves.

Black Tea

Black teas are fully oxidized teas. Black teas brew a liquor from reddish brown to dark brown.
